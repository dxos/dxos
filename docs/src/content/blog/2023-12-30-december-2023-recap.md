## Updates

### We will be at the Effect Conference!

Jess Martin will be speaking at the first ever [Effect Conference](https://effect.website/events/effect-days). Explore the power of the Effect ecosystem and community with us in Vienna, Austria on February 22-24.

## Knowledge Sharing

### devtools FM podcast

On December 10, Jess Martin spoke about redefining apps and data on the 78th episode of the [devtools FM podcast](https://www.devtools.fm/). Jess shared about his career path, and showcased how DXOS is reimagining the shape of software. The discussion with Andrew and Justin covers the challenges of building sustainable businesses in the open-source space, with DXOS exploring revenue streams through services like offsite backup solutions. Integrating payment primitives into the platform was also discussed, to facilitate developers getting paid for their work. Listen to the full length interview [here](https://www.devtools.fm/episode/78), or read the transcript [here](https://www.devtools.fm/episode/78?view=TRANSCRIPT).
